National Anthem Day

Executive Order

Whereby it is declared upon the completion of a new turn by the Salanda National Symphony Orchestra for Salanda's National Anthem called 'Our Great Kingdom'

On the 9th of August of two thousand twenty-two that from now on, anually, that the ninenth of August shall be a special national holiday with all businesses institutions that are not needed and education closed with the Salanda National anthem Day on the 9th of August celebratiing the new tune of Salanda's National Anthem, 'Our Kindong'.

Ruben I, Incumbent Monarch of the Kingdom of Salanda

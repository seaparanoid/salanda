ACT OF THE TEPOLEAN

Oficialisation Of The Salandarian Voting System

This bill when passed shall oficialise the Salandarian voting system for elections. With reference to the Salandarian Constitution (known as Basic Foundation Laws) which states the list system as the voting system, but does not reference a proper system of how voting shall be conducted that shall be followed though however the First Past the Post (abbreviated as FPTP) voting system and the Proportional Representation (abbreviated as PR) voting system have been used for different elections accross (the Kingdom of) Salanda since its democratisation in late 2020. This bill when passed shall officialise the voting system for the different types of elections that are held in (the Kingdom of) Salanda.

The First Past the Post FPTP voting system shall be used in by-elections in Salanda where one seat in the Tepolean in the by-election which are safe for an individual seat that are contested by multiple parties to produce a clear winner in the by-election of which the by-election shall not end until one party has more votes that the other parties it shall be declared the winner even if the other parties have more votes combined than the party which has more votes individually since this is how FPTP operates.

The Proportional Representation PR system shall be used in the General Elections of (the Kingdom of) Salanda that are held every two years normally but can be less if anything arises that causes the Government to collapse such as a Vote of No Confidence motion. In these General Elections where all the seats in the Tepolean are up for an election where the next Government is formed each Party shall receive a sear or a number of seats proportional to how many votes it gets.

Ruben I, Incumbent Monarch of the Kingdom of Salanda declare my assent for the Oficialisation of the Salandarian Voting System, as finally passed by the Tepolean on 18 July 2022
